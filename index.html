Introduction to the course

  • Process engineering is the design of chemical processes, onshore examples include bread/chemical/pharmeceutical production.
  • Offshore process engineering includes oil & gas extraction, CO2/H2 purification and injection for storage, mineral and geothermal extraction.
  • These are some of the most constrained/tough environments to work in, so we see some of the best of Process Engineering.
  • This course revisits many basic Process Engineering topics (thermodynamics, flow, separation, safety, efficiency), but in an offshore context.
  • This is more applied, and covers advanced aspects, but most calculations are short-cut approaches.
